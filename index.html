Butterfly effect

  • Press space to release a sphere at (8, 5, 13) + some noise

  • Press left/right arrow to change the camera x position

  • Press up/down arrow to change the camera y position

  • Press left/right arrow while holding ctrl to change the camera z position

  • Press x/ctrl + x to increase/decrease camera rotation in the x-axis

  • Press y/ctrl + y to increase/decrease camera rotation in the y-axis

  • Press z/ctrl + z to increase/decrease camera rotation in the z-axis
